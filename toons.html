About Toons

Common Toons

These Toons are more abundantly produced than the rest! They tend to have a lot more variation between individuals due to this, especially visually.

Uncommon Toons

These Toons are slightly rarer to be produced than Common Toons, but still are abundant enough to show some level of variation between individuals.

Rare Toons

These Toons are not produced as often, and rarely has any variation right out the gate. Most variations in these Toons are modified after production.

Main Character Toons

The rarest Toons to be produced and released publicly! Almost all are perfect replicas of their originals, any further variations are modified after distribution.

Event Toons

These Toons are exclusively obtained during events! Because so few are made during a year, most are near-perfect replicas of the originals, however variations still occur.

Lethal Toons

These Toons are rarely produced, and rarely meets the eyes of the public; besides Dandy, the mascot of this establishment.

Statuses

Twisteds

Twisteds are Toons that have been overcome with ichor, often losing control of their body and become aggressive when provoked. Some Twisteds can be docile upon initial contact, but are still dangerous when angered. There are ways to revert Twisteds back to their normal selves, albeit with some scars.

Terminated

These Toons/Twisteds are terminated, and can no longer be used to go on runs. Their death is permanent and cannot be reversed under any circumstances. Think wisely before terminating your Toons.
